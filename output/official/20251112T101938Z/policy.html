Data Subject Rights

As a data subject, you have various rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) that enable you to control your personal data and how it is processed. At Privact P.C., we respect these rights and are committed to providing you with clear information about them.

Enabled Rights

The following rights are enabled for our organization:

  • Access: You have the right to access your personal data, which includes obtaining a copy of the data we hold.
  • Rectification: If you believe that any of your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request us to rectify it.
  • Erasure: In certain circumstances, you may request us to erase your personal data. This right is not absolute and only applies in specific situations where the data is no longer necessary for its original purpose.
  • Restriction: You have the right to restrict our processing of your personal data if you contest its accuracy or object to its processing.
  • Portability: If we process your personal data by automated means, you can request us to provide it in a structured and commonly used format so that you can transfer it to another controller.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or where such processing is based on legitimate interests.
  • Automated Decisions: If we make automated decisions about you, including profiling, you have the right to request human intervention and to challenge any decision made.

Additional Rights

Our organization also recognizes additional rights that are not part of the GDPR core rights:

  • Data Portability: You can request us to provide your personal data in a structured format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we rely on consent as our lawful basis for processing, you have the right to withdraw it at any time.

Submitting Requests

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please follow these steps:

  1. Write to us via email: Contact our DPO via the published privacy email.
  2. Provide your name and contact details so we can verify your identity.
  3. Clearly state which right you are exercising and provide any additional information required.

Response Timelines

We will respond to all requests within one month from receipt of your request. However, this period may be extended by two months if the request is complex or requires further verification. We will inform you in writing if we need more time and explain why.

Verification Steps

To verify your identity, we may require additional information or documentation. This is to ensure that we only provide access to your personal data to authorized individuals.
