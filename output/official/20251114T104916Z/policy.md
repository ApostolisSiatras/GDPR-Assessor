## Data Subject Rights

As a data subject, you have various rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) that allow you to control how your personal data is processed. At Redacted Organisation, we respect and support these rights, ensuring transparency in our practices.

### Core GDPR Rights

We enable the following core rights as per the GDPR:

1. **Access**: You have the right to access your personal data held by us.
2. **Rectification**: If you believe any of your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, we will rectify it upon request.
3. **Erasure**: In certain circumstances, you may request erasure of your personal data from our records.
4. **Restriction**: You can restrict the processing of your personal data in specific situations.
5. **Portability**: We facilitate the transfer of your personal data to another controller where technically feasible.
6. **Objection**: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or other legitimate interests.
7. **Automated Decisions**: If we make decisions based on automated processes, you can request information about these processes and their logic.

### Additional Rights

We also support additional rights as per our organisational policies:

1. **Right to Data Portability**: We facilitate the transfer of your personal data to another controller where technically feasible.
2. **Right to Object**: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or other legitimate interests.
