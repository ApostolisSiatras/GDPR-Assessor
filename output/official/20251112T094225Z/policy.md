## Data Subject Rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

* Access: The right to access your personal data that we process.
* Rectification: The right to correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
* Erasure: The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
* Restriction: The right to restrict our processing of your personal data.
* Portability: The right to receive a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
* Objection: The right to object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or other legitimate interests.
* Automated decisions: The right not to be subject to automated decision-making, including profiling.

### Breach Response Timeline

In the event of a personal data breach, our organization will follow the procedures outlined in Articles 33/34 of the GDPR. The following timeline outlines the key steps:

1. **Notification**: Within 72 hours of becoming aware of a breach, we will notify the relevant authorities and affected individuals.
2. **Assessment**: We will conduct an assessment to determine the scope and impact of the breach.
3. **Communication**: We will communicate with affected individuals and provide them with information about the breach, including the nature of the personal data involved, the steps taken to mitigate the breach, and any measures being taken to prevent similar breaches in the future.
4. **Investigation**: We will conduct a thorough investigation into the cause of the breach and implement corrective actions to prevent similar incidents.

When relevant, we will also notify our processors and transfers as required by Article 33(2) of the GDPR.
