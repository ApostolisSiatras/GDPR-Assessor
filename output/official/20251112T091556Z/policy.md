## Security and Incident Response

### Preventive and Detective Controls

Our organization is committed to implementing proportionate security measures to protect personal data. While our current security measures are limited, we recognize the importance of proactive controls to prevent and detect potential breaches.

We commit to implementing the following preventive and detective controls:

* Regular security audits and risk assessments
* Implementation of access controls and authentication mechanisms
* Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
* Secure protocols for data transfer (e.g., HTTPS)
* Incident response plan and training for personnel

### Breach Response Timeline

In the event of a personal data breach, our organization will follow the procedures outlined below:

1. **Notification**: Within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, we will notify the relevant authorities (Article 33) and, if required, the affected individuals.
2. **Assessment**: We will conduct an assessment to determine the scope and impact of the breach.
3. **Containment**: We will take immediate action to contain the breach and prevent further unauthorized access or disclosure.
4. **Notification of processors/transfers**: If we engage with third-party processors or transfers data outside the EU, we will notify them in accordance with our contracts and applicable laws (Article 34).
5. **Communication with affected individuals**: We will provide clear and transparent communication to affected individuals regarding the breach, its impact, and any measures taken to mitigate it.
6. **Review and improvement**: We will conduct a thorough review of the incident and implement improvements to prevent similar breaches in the future.
