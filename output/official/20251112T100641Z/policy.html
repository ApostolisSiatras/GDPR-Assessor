Data Subject Rights

As a data subject, you have several rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) that are outlined below. These rights can be exercised by submitting a request to our organization.

Enabled Rights

The following rights are enabled in accordance with our GDPR policy:

* Access: You have the right to access your personal data.

* Rectification: You have the right to rectify any inaccuracies or incomplete information.

* Erasure: You have the right to erasure of your personal data under certain conditions.

* Restriction: You have the right to restrict processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

* Portability: You have the right to receive a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

* Objection: You have the right to object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or other legitimate interests.

* Automated Decisions: You have the right to object to automated decision-making, including profiling.

Breach Response Timeline

In the event of a personal data breach, our organization will follow the timeline outlined below:

  1. Detection: Our organization will detect the breach as soon as possible.
  2. Notification to DPO and Management: The Data Protection Officer (DPO) and management will be notified immediately.
  3. Assessment of Breach: A thorough assessment of the breach will be conducted, including identifying the cause, impact, and scope of the breach.
  4. Notification to Supervisory Authority: Within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, our organization will notify the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA) in accordance with Article 33 of the GDPR.
  5. Notification to Affected Individuals: Our organization will also notify affected individuals without undue delay and in accordance with Article 34 of the GDPR.
  6. Communication with Processors/Transfers: If a processor or transfer is involved, our organization will communicate with them as necessary to ensure that they are aware of the breach and take any necessary steps to mitigate its impact.
