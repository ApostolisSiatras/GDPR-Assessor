# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by PRIVACT PC.

## Retention Schedule
The following data categories will be retained for the specified duration:

* **Account information**: 5 years (as per ctx["retention"]["account"])
* **Communication records**: 2 years (as per ctx["retention"]["communication"])
* **Financial transactions**: 7 years (as per ctx["retention"]["financial"])
* **Marketing data**: 1 year (as per ctx["retention"]["marketing"])

## Lawful Bases & Justification
The lawful bases for processing personal data are:

* Consent: obtained from individuals through explicit opt-in
* Legitimate interests: processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of PRIVACT PC, such as improving services and security

Justification for retention periods:
Retention periods are based on legal requirements, industry standards, and business needs.

## Storage & Security
Personal data will be stored in a secure manner, using encryption and access controls. Data will be processed by authorized personnel only.

## Deletion & Destruction
Data will be deleted or destroyed when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. This includes:

* **Account information**: deleted after 5 years (as per ctx["retention"]["account"])
* **Communication records**: deleted after 2 years (as per ctx["retention"]["communication"])
* **Financial transactions**: deleted after 7 years (as per ctx["retention"]["financial"])
