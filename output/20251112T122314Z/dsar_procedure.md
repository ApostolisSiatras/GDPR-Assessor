# DSAR Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Purpose
This procedure outlines the steps to be taken when handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Intake & Verification

1. Identify and verify all incoming DSAR requests, ensuring they are legitimate and comply with GDPR requirements.
2. Confirm the identity of the data subject making the request to ensure their rights can be exercised.
3. Determine if the request is valid by checking for completeness and accuracy.

## Assessment & Coordination

1. Assess the scope of the request and identify the relevant personal data held by PRIVACT PC.
2. Coordinate with internal teams, including IT and Data Protection Officer (DPO), to gather necessary information.
3. Evaluate the lawful bases for processing the requested data, referencing [lawful_bases] from our records.

## Response Content

1. Provide a clear and concise response to the data subject, outlining:
	* The personal data held by PRIVACT PC
	* The purposes of processing this data
	* Any third-party processors involved (referencing [processors])
	* Details on transfers of personal data outside the EU/EEA (referencing [transfers])
2. Include information on retention periods for the requested data, referencing [retention] from our records.
3. Clearly outline the rights of the data subject and how they can be exercised.

## Timelines & Escalations

1. Respond to DSAR requests within 30 days of receipt, as per GDPR requirements.
2. Escalate complex or high-priority requests to the DPO for further guidance and support.
3. Monitor progress and ensure timely completion of all tasks related to DSAR handling.

## Recordkeeping

1. Maintain accurate records of all DSAR requests, including:
	* Request details
	* Response content
	* Communication with data subjects
2. Store these records securely and in accordance with our [retention] policies.
