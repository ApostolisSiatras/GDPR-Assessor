Here is the Markdown incident response runbook for personal data breaches:
```
# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

When a potential personal data breach is detected, follow these steps:

1. **Notify the DPO**: Inform the Data Protection Officer (DPO) of the suspected breach as soon as possible.
2. **Gather information**:
	* Identify the source and nature of the breach
	* Determine the types of personal data involved
	* Assess the potential impact on individuals and the organization
3. **Activate incident response plan**: Engage the incident response team to contain and mitigate the breach.

## Containment

To prevent further damage, take the following steps:

1. **Isolate affected systems**:
	* Disconnect affected systems from the network
	* Disable access to sensitive data
2. **Notify processors and transfers**:
	* Inform any third-party processors or transfer recipients of the breach
	* Request their assistance in containing the breach
3. **Review retention policies**: Check if personal data is being retained for longer than necessary

## Assessment & Notification

Assess the scope and impact of the breach, and notify affected parties:

1. **Conduct a thorough investigation**:
	* Identify the root cause of the breach
	* Determine the extent of the breach
2. **Notify affected individuals**: Inform individuals whose personal data has been compromised
3. **Notify regulatory authorities**: Report the breach to relevant regulatory bodies (e.g., ICO, DPA)
4. **Inform lawful bases and cookies**:
	* Review the lawful bases for processing personal data
	* Check if cookies are being used in accordance with regulations

## Communication

Communicate with stakeholders and affected parties:

1. **Internal communication**: Inform employees and management of the breach
2. **External communication**: Provide information to affected individuals, regulatory authorities, and other relevant parties
3. **Security measures review**: Review security measures in place to prevent similar breaches in the future

## Post-incident Review

Conduct a thorough review of the incident:

1. **Document lessons learned**:
	* Identify areas for improvement
	* Document changes made to prevent similar breaches
2. **Review security measures**:
	* Check if security measures are adequate and effective
3. **Update policies and procedures**: Update policies and procedures to reflect lessons learned
```
