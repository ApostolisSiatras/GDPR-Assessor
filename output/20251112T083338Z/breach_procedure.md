# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

When a personal data breach is suspected or detected:

1. **Notify the DPO**: Inform the Data Protection Officer (DPO) immediately, providing as much detail as possible about the incident.
2. **Gather information**:
	* Identify the type and scope of the breach
	* Determine the potential impact on individuals' personal data
	* Review relevant documentation, including processing registers and retention policies
3. **Assess the risk**: Evaluate the likelihood and potential consequences of the breach using the provided risk assessment framework

## Containment

To prevent further unauthorized access or disclosure:

1. **Isolate affected systems**: Immediately disconnect or isolate any systems or networks involved in the breach to prevent further data compromise.
2. **Disable cookies**:
	* If cookies are enabled, disable them to prevent potential tracking or profiling
3. **Review transfers**:
	* Check if any sensitive personal data has been transferred to third-party processors or vendors
4. **Implement security measures**:
	* Activate relevant security measures, such as encryption or access controls, to protect affected systems and data

## Assessment & Notification

To determine the scope of the breach and notify affected parties:

1. **Conduct a thorough investigation**: Gather evidence and interview relevant personnel to understand the cause and extent of the breach.
2. **Assess lawful bases**:
	* Review the lawful bases for processing personal data, as specified in the processing register
3. **Notify affected individuals**:
	* Inform individuals whose personal data has been compromised, providing details about the breach and any recommended actions
4. **Notify regulatory authorities**: If required by law or regulation, notify relevant authorities, such as the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

## Communication

To maintain transparency and cooperation:

1. **Internal communication**:
	* Inform all relevant personnel about the breach and containment efforts
2. **External communication**:
	* Provide updates to affected individuals and regulatory authorities
3. **Media statement**: Prepare a media statement to address public concerns, if necessary

## Post-incident Review

To identify lessons learned and improve incident response:

1. **Conduct a thorough review**: Analyze the breach and containment efforts to identify areas for improvement.
2. **Document findings**:
	* Record lessons learned and recommendations for future improvements
3. **Update procedures**: Revise the personal data breach procedure based on the review's findings
