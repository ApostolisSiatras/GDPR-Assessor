# DSAR Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Purpose
This procedure outlines the steps to be taken when handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Intake & Verification
1. Identify and verify all incoming DSAR requests.
2. Confirm the identity of the data subject making the request.
3. Ensure that the request is made in writing, either by email or letter.

## Assessment & Coordination
1. Review the request to determine if it falls within our organization's scope.
2. Assess the validity and completeness of the request.
3. Coordinate with relevant departments (e.g., IT, HR) as necessary.
4. Identify any applicable lawful bases from the [lawful_bases](#lawful_bases) register.

## Response Content
1. Provide a clear and concise response to the data subject's request.
2. Include all requested information, including:
	* Personal data processed by our organization.
	* Categories of personal data.
	* Recipients or categories of recipients of the personal data.
	* Any transfers to third countries or international organizations.
3. Reference any applicable safeguards from the [processors](#processors), [transfers](#transfers), and [retention](#retention) registers.

## Timelines & Escalations
1. Respond to DSAR requests within 30 days of receipt, unless an extension is necessary due to complexity or other circumstances.
2. Escalate complex or high-priority requests to the Data Protection Officer (DPO).

## Recordkeeping
1. Maintain accurate and detailed records of all DSAR requests and responses.
2. Store records securely in accordance with our [retention](#retention) policy.
