# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by PRIVACT.

## Retention Schedule
The following data categories will be retained for the specified duration:

* **Customer Information**: 5 years (retention period)
	+ Customer contact details
	+ Order history
* **Transaction Data**: 3 years (retention period)
	+ Payment information
	+ Transaction records
* **Marketing Data**: 2 years (retention period)
	+ Marketing campaign data
	+ Lead generation data

## Lawful Bases & Justification
PRIVACT relies on the following lawful bases for processing personal data:

* Consent: Customers provide explicit consent for processing their personal data.
* Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to fulfill contractual obligations.

Justification:
The retention of personal data is necessary to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and to maintain business operations.

## Storage & Security
Personal data will be stored on secure servers located in [insert location]. Access to the servers will be restricted to authorized personnel only. Data protection measures include:

* Encryption: All personal data will be encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols.
* Secure Protocols: Only secure communication protocols (HTTPS) will be used for transmitting and receiving personal data.

## Deletion & Destruction
Personal data will be deleted or destroyed in accordance with the retention schedule outlined above. When deletion is not possible, data will be anonymized to prevent identification.
