# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

When a potential personal data breach is detected, follow these steps:

1. **Initial Response**
	* Identify the source of the breach (e.g., processor, transfer)
	* Determine the scope and severity of the breach
	* Notify relevant stakeholders (e.g., DPO, management)
2. **Triage**
	* Assess the risk level of the breach using the provided risk assessment framework
	* Evaluate the likelihood and impact of the breach on personal data

## Containment

To contain the breach and prevent further damage:

1. **Immediate Action**
	* Suspend all processing activities related to the affected personal data
	* Notify processors and transfers involved in the breach
2. **Containment Measures**
	* Implement temporary security measures to prevent unauthorized access (e.g., blocking IP addresses)
	* Review and update security measures as necessary

## Assessment & Notification

To assess the impact of the breach and notify relevant parties:

1. **Breach Assessment**
	* Evaluate the scope, severity, and risk level of the breach
	* Determine the lawful bases for processing affected personal data (refer to ctx.lawful_bases)
2. **Notification**
	* Notify affected individuals about the breach (if required by law or regulation)
	* Inform relevant stakeholders, including processors and transfers

## Communication

To communicate with affected parties and stakeholders:

1. **Breach Notification Letter**
	* Provide a clear explanation of the breach and its impact
	* Outline steps taken to contain and mitigate the breach
2. **Stakeholder Communication**
	* Notify DPO, management, and relevant stakeholders about the breach
	* Provide regular updates on the incident response efforts

## Post-incident Review

To review and improve incident response processes:

1. **Post-Incident Review Meeting**
	* Conduct a thorough review of the incident response efforts
	* Identify areas for improvement in detection, containment, assessment, and communication
2. **Action Plan**
	* Develop an action plan to implement changes and improvements
	* Monitor progress and report back to stakeholders
