# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by DOTSOFT. It outlines the procedures for retaining, storing, and deleting personal data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## Retention Schedule
The following categories of personal data will be retained for the specified duration:

* **Customer Data**: 5 years (retention.duration.customer)
* **Transaction Data**: 3 years (retention.duration.transaction)
* **Marketing Data**: 2 years (retention.duration.marketing)

Retention durations are subject to change and may be updated as necessary.

## Lawful Bases & Justification
DOTSOFT processes personal data on the following lawful bases:

* Consent: Customers have provided explicit consent for processing their personal data.
* Contract: Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract between DOTSOFT and its customers.
* Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of DOTSOFT, such as improving services and marketing.
