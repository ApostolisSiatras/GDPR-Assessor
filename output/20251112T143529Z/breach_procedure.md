Here is the Markdown incident response runbook for personal data breaches:
```markdown
# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

1. **Initial Response**
	* Identify potential breach through monitoring and detection tools
	* Verify the breach using available information (e.g., processor notifications, customer reports)
2. **Triage**
	* Assess the scope of the breach (number of records affected, type of data exposed)
	* Determine the likelihood and potential impact of the breach on individuals

## Containment

1. **Immediate Action**
	* Notify processors and vendors involved in the breach
	* Take steps to prevent further unauthorized access or exfiltration of personal data
2. **Containment Measures**
	* Review security measures in place for processing and retention of personal data (refer to `security_measures` and `retention`)
	* Assess the effectiveness of cookies and lawful bases used for processing (refer to `cookies` and `lawful_bases`)

## Assessment & Notification

1. **Breach Assessment**
	* Determine the root cause of the breach
	* Evaluate the likelihood and potential impact of the breach on individuals
2. **Notification**
	* Notify affected individuals, including those whose personal data may have been exposed (refer to `transfers`)
	* Provide information about the breach, including the type of data exposed and steps taken to prevent future breaches

## Communication

1. **Internal Communication**
	* Inform relevant stakeholders within the organization, including management and employees
	* Provide guidance on how to respond to affected individuals and media inquiries
2. **External Communication**
	* Publish a public notice about the breach (if required by law or regulation)
	* Respond to media inquiries and provide updates on the status of the incident

## Post-incident Review

1. **Review and Analysis**
	* Conduct a thorough review of the breach, including root cause analysis
	* Identify areas for improvement in security measures, retention, and lawful bases (refer to `security_measures`, `retention`, and `lawful_bases`)
2. **Recommendations and Actions**
	* Develop recommendations for improving incident response processes and preventing future breaches
	* Implement changes to security measures, retention, and lawful bases as necessary

## Contact

DOTSOFT (Email: DOTSOFT)
```
Note that this is just a sample runbook and should be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure it remains relevant and effective.
