# DSAR Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Purpose
This procedure outlines the steps to be taken when handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) in accordance with relevant data protection regulations.

## Intake & Verification
### Step 1: Receive and Acknowledge Request
* The DSAR should be received by the designated contact person (Email: DOTSOFT).
* The request should be acknowledged promptly, ideally within 24 hours.
* Verify that the request is legitimate and not a duplicate.

### Step 2: Identify Relevant Data
* Determine which data is relevant to the request and identify any potential exemptions.
* Consult with relevant departments or teams as necessary.

## Assessment & Coordination
### Step 3: Assess Lawful Bases
* Review the lawful bases for processing personal data (ctx.lawful_bases).
* Ensure that the requested data was processed on a valid basis.

### Step 4: Identify Processors and Transfers
* Identify any processors involved in the processing of the requested data (ctx.processors).
* Determine if any transfers have taken place (ctx.transfers).

## Response Content
### Step 5: Compile Requested Data
* Gather all relevant data requested by the individual.
* Ensure that the data is accurate and up-to-date.

### Step 6: Provide Information on Safeguards
* Include information on the safeguards in place, including:
	+ Lawful bases (ctx.lawful_bases)
	+ Processors involved (ctx.processors)
	+ Transfers made (ctx.transfers)
	+ Retention periods (ctx.retention)
	+ Rights of the individual (ctx.sections.E.Rights)
	+ Security controls in place (ctx.security_measures)

## Timelines & Escalations
### Step 7: Establish Timeline for Response
* Aim to respond within 30 days, as per relevant regulations.
* Establish a clear timeline for completion and communicate it to the individual.

### Step 8: Escalate if Necessary
* If additional time is required, escalate the request to management or the Data Protection Officer (DPO).

## Recordkeeping
### Step 9: Maintain Accurate Records
* Keep accurate records of all DSARs received, including:
	+ Date and time of receipt
	+ Details of the request
	+ Response provided

## Contact
For any questions or concerns regarding this procedure, please contact Email: DOTSOFT.
