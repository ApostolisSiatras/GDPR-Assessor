# DSAR Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Purpose
This procedure outlines the steps to be taken when handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Intake & Verification
1. Identify and verify the requestor's identity.
2. Confirm that the request is a DSAR under GDPR.
3. Check if the request is valid and complete.

### Safeguards

* Lawful bases: The organisation operates on the basis of legitimate interests, as specified in the lawful_bases section of the ctx.
* Processors: The organisation uses processors for data processing, as listed in the processors section of the ctx.
* Transfers: The organisation transfers personal data to third countries, as outlined in the transfers section of the ctx.
* Retention: Personal data is retained in accordance with the retention policy, as specified in the retention section of the ctx.

## Assessment & Coordination
1. Determine the scope of the request and identify relevant data.
2. Assess the risk associated with the request.
3. Coordinate with relevant departments to gather necessary information.

### Safeguards

* Rights: The organisation respects the rights of data subjects, as outlined in the sections section of the ctx (E.Rights).
* Security controls: The organisation maintains robust security measures, as listed in the security_measures section of the ctx.

## Response Content
1. Provide a clear and concise response to the requestor.
2. Include all relevant information, including data processed and retention periods.
3. Ensure that the response is accurate and complete.

### Safeguards

* Lawful bases: The organisation's lawful bases for processing are clearly stated in the response.
* Retention: The organisation's retention policy is explained in the response.

## Timelines & Escalations
1. Establish a timeline for responding to the request.
2. Identify potential escalation points and procedures.

### Safeguards

* Risk rating: The organisation's risk rating, as specified in the risk section of the ctx, informs the handling of DSARs.

## Recordkeeping
1. Maintain accurate records of all DSARs received.
2. Store records securely and for a minimum period of 5 years.

### Safeguards

* Security measures: The organisation's security measures, as listed in the security_measures section of the ctx, protect against unauthorised access to records.

## Contact
For further information or assistance with DSARs, please contact [Data protection contact available upon request.](mailto:data.protection@organisation.com)
