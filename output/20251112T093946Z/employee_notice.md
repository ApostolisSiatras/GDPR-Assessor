# Employee Privacy Notice
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Overview
We are committed to protecting your personal data. This notice explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data.

## Categories of Personal Data
We may collect the following categories of personal data:
* Contact information (e.g., email address)
* Job title and role
* Work-related performance data

## Lawful Bases
The lawful bases for processing your personal data are:
* Performance of a contract (e.g., employment contract)
* Compliance with a legal obligation (e.g., tax laws)

## International Transfers
We may transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) to countries that do not provide an adequate level of protection. We will only make such transfers where necessary and in compliance with applicable laws.

## Retention
We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill our contractual or legal obligations, or until you request us to delete it.

## Employee Rights
You have the following rights:
* Right to access your personal data
* Right to correct inaccurate personal data
* Right to erase your personal data (in certain circumstances)
* Right to restrict processing of your personal data
* Right to object to processing of your personal data

## Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect your personal data, including:
* Encryption
* Access controls
* Regular backups
