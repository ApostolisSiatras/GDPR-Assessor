Here is the Markdown incident response runbook for personal data breaches:
```
# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

1. **Initial Response**
	* Identify potential breach through monitoring or notification (e.g., from processors, transfers, or lawful bases)
	* Verify the incident and assess its impact on personal data
2. **Triage Checklist**
	* Review cookies usage and settings
	* Check retention policies for affected data
	* Assess security measures in place to prevent similar incidents

## Containment

1. **Isolate Affected Systems**
	* Immediately isolate systems or networks that may be compromised
	* Prevent further access to personal data
2. **Notify Processors & Vendors**
	* Inform processors and vendors about the breach, if applicable
3. **Check Transfers & Lawful Bases**
	* Review transfers and lawful bases for affected data

## Assessment & Notification

1. **Assess Impact & Scope**
	* Determine the extent of the breach (e.g., number of individuals affected)
	* Assess potential risks to personal data
2. **Notify Affected Individuals**
	* Provide clear and concise notification about the breach, including:
		+ Description of what happened
		+ Types of personal data involved
		+ Steps taken to contain the breach
		+ Contact information for further questions or concerns
3. **Notification Checklist**
	* Review cookies usage and settings
	* Check retention policies for affected data

## Communication

1. **Internal Communication**
	* Inform relevant stakeholders about the breach, including:
		+ Data protection contact
		+ DPO (if applicable)
2. **External Communication**
	* Provide updates to affected individuals and regulatory bodies (e.g., ICO)

## Post-incident Review

1. **Review & Analysis**
	* Conduct thorough review of the incident, including:
		+ Root cause analysis
		+ Lessons learned
		+ Recommendations for improvement
2. **Update Procedures & Policies**
	* Update procedures and policies to prevent similar incidents in the future

## Contact

* Data protection contact: [Data protection contact available upon request]
* DPO (if applicable): [DPO contact information]
```
Note that I've referenced processors, transfers, lawful bases, cookies, retention, and security measures from the provided context only.
