# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by Redacted Organisation.

## Retention Schedule
The following categories of data will be retained for the specified durations:

* **Customer Information**: 5 years (ctx["retention"]["customer_info"])
* **Transaction Data**: 3 years (ctx["retention"]["transaction_data"])
* **Marketing Data**: 1 year (ctx["retention"]["marketing_data"])

## Lawful Bases & Justification
The organisation relies on the following lawful bases for processing personal data:

* Consent: Customers provide explicit consent for marketing communications.
* Contract: Personal data is processed to fulfill contractual obligations.
* Legitimate Interests: The organisation has a legitimate interest in processing personal data for customer service and improvement purposes.

## Deletion & Destruction
Personal data will be deleted after the retention period has expired. The organisation reserves the right to retain data for longer periods where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

**Rights of Erasure and Objection**

* Customers have the right to erasure of their personal data, except where this is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation.
* Customers have the right to object to processing of their personal data for marketing purposes.
