# DSAR Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Purpose
This procedure outlines the steps to be taken when handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

## Intake & Verification
1. Identify and acknowledge receipt of a DSAR from an individual.
2. Verify the identity of the requester using the information provided.
3. Check if the request is complete and includes all necessary details.

## Assessment & Coordination
1. Determine the lawful basis for processing the personal data requested, referencing [lawful bases](#lawful_bases) in ctx.
2. Identify any processors involved in the processing of the data, as listed in [processors](#processors) in ctx.
3. Assess if any transfers of personal data have occurred, referencing [transfers](#transfers) in ctx.
4. Determine the retention period for the requested data, referencing [retention](#retention) in ctx.

## Response Content
1. Provide a clear and concise response to the requester, including:
	* A description of the personal data processed.
	* The purposes for which the data was collected and processed.
	* Any third parties with whom the data has been shared.
	* Details on how the individual's rights have been exercised.
2. Include information on applicable security controls, such as encryption and access controls.

## Timelines & Escalations
1. Respond to DSARs within [30 calendar days](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/guide-to-data-protection/guide-to-the-general-data-protection-regulation-gdpr/data-subject-requests/) of receipt.
2. Escalate complex or high-priority requests to the Data Protection Officer (DPO) or designated team.

## Recordkeeping
1. Maintain a record of all DSARs received, including:
	* Requester's details and contact information.
	* Date and time of receipt.
	* Response provided.
2. Store records securely in accordance with [retention](#retention) policies.
