# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by DOTSOFT.

## Retention Schedule
The following data categories will be retained for the specified duration:

* **Customer information**: 5 years (as per ctx["retention"]["customer"])
* **Transaction records**: 3 years (as per ctx["retention"]["transaction"])
* **Marketing data**: 1 year (as per ctx["retention"]["marketing"])

## Lawful Bases & Justification
The lawful bases for processing personal data are:

* Consent: Customers have given explicit consent to process their data.
* Legitimate interests: Processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of DOTSOFT, such as improving services and communicating with customers.

Justification:
Data will be retained in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. The retention schedule above reflects the minimum period required to achieve the purposes for which the data was collected.

## Storage & Security
Personal data will be stored on secure servers located within the European Economic Area (EEA). Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

## Deletion & Destruction
Data will be deleted or destroyed when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. The following procedures will be followed:

* **Customer information**: Data will be deleted 5 years after the customer's last interaction with DOTSOFT.
* **Transaction records**: Data will be deleted 3 years after the transaction date.
* **Marketing data**: Data will be deleted 1 year after the marketing campaign ends.

## Roles & Contact
The following roles are responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy:

* Data Protection Officer (DPO): NO
* Contact: Email: DOTSOFT

Rights of individuals:
Customers have the right to erasure and objection. Requests should be sent to the contact email address above.

Note: This policy does not apply to cookies, as they are disabled on our website due to lack of website presence.
