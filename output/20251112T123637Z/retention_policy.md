# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by Redacted Organisation.

## Retention Schedule
The following categories of personal data will be retained for a period of **1 month**:

* Cookies: Not enabled, but if enabled in the future, will be retained for 1 month.
* User interactions and behavior data (not specified in ctx)
* Other data categories not explicitly mentioned in ctx

## Lawful Bases & Justification
The following lawful bases have been declared:

* Consent
* Contract
* Legal Obligation

Data is processed on the basis of these lawful bases, as per the organisation's legitimate interests.

## Storage & Security
Personal data will be stored securely using industry-standard measures. The organisation has appointed no Data Protection Officer (DPO) and has not specified an establishment country.

## Deletion & Destruction
After the retention period, personal data will be deleted or destroyed in accordance with the organisation's data protection policies.

## Roles & Contact
The following roles are responsible for data protection:

* Data protection contact: available upon request.
* DPO: Not appointed
