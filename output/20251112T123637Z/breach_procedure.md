# Personal Data Breach Procedure
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Detection & Triage

1. **Initial Response**
	* Identify potential breach through monitoring and detection tools
	* Verify breach using contextual information (e.g., processor logs, transfer records)
2. **Triage Checklist**
	* Has a personal data breach occurred?
		+ Yes: Proceed to Containment section
		+ No: Continue monitoring and re-evaluate
3. **Notify Relevant Parties**
	* Inform Data Protection Officer (DPO) or designated contact
	* Notify relevant stakeholders, including processors and transfer recipients

## Containment

1. **Immediate Action**
	* Disable access to affected systems and data
	* Isolate affected networks and devices
2. **Containment Checklist**
	* Have cookies been enabled on the website?
		+ Yes: Disable cookies immediately
		+ No: Proceed to Assessment & Notification section
3. **Notify Affected Parties**
	* Inform processors, transfer recipients, and other relevant parties

## Assessment & Notification

1. **Assessment Checklist**
	* What type of personal data was breached? (e.g., sensitive, non-sensitive)
	* How many individuals were affected?
	* Was the breach caused by a processor or transfer recipient?
2. **Notification Requirements**
	* Notify affected individuals within [Retention] months
	* Provide information on the breach, including:
		+ Type of data breached
		+ Number of individuals affected
		+ Measures taken to prevent future breaches

## Communication

1. **Internal Communication**
	* Inform all relevant stakeholders, including employees and management
	* Provide regular updates on the incident response process
2. **External Communication**
	* Notify affected individuals through email or postal mail
	* Publish a public notice on the website (if applicable)

## Post-incident Review

1. **Review Checklist**
	* What caused the breach?
	* Were security measures in place to prevent the breach?
	* How can future breaches be prevented?
2. **Action Plan**
	* Develop an action plan to implement changes and improvements
	* Schedule regular reviews to ensure compliance with regulations

* Designated contact: [Insert contact information]

Note:
* This procedure is based on the provided context and may require additional steps or modifications depending on specific organizational needs.
