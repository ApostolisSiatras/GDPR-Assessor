# Data Retention Policy
Last updated: 2025-11-12

## Scope
This data retention policy applies to all personal data processed by PRIVACT P.C. (the "Organization") in the course of its business activities.

## Retention Schedule
The following categories of personal data will be retained for the specified periods:

* **Customer Data**: 5 years from last interaction (ctx["retention"]["customer_data"])
* **Transaction Data**: 3 years from transaction date (ctx["retention"]["transaction_data"])
* **Employee Data**: 2 years from employment termination (ctx["retention"]["employee_data"])

## Lawful Bases & Justification
The Organization relies on the following lawful bases for processing personal data:

* Consent: Where individuals have provided explicit consent to process their personal data.
* Contractual Necessity: Where processing is necessary to fulfill contractual obligations.
* Legitimate Interests: Where processing is necessary to pursue legitimate interests of the Organization.

## Storage & Security
Personal data will be stored on secure servers located in [insert location]. The Organization has implemented appropriate security measures, including:

* Encryption of personal data at rest and in transit
* Access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access personal data

The Organization uses the following processors for certain categories of personal data:

* **Cloud Storage Provider**: For storing customer data (ctx["processors"]["cloud_storage_provider"])

## Deletion & Destruction
Personal data will be deleted or destroyed when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. The Organization will ensure that all personal data is properly deleted or destroyed in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## Roles & Contact
The following roles are responsible for ensuring compliance with this data retention policy:

* **Data Protection Officer (DPO)**: Christos Kalloniatis (ctx["org"]["dpo"])
* **Contact**: Email: [Christos Kalloniatis](ctx["org"]["contact"])

Individuals have the right to erasure and objection, as follows:

* **Right to Erasure**: Individuals can request that their personal data be deleted at any time.
* **Right to Object**: Individuals can object to processing of their personal data for direct marketing purposes.

Note: The Organization will respond to all requests within 30 days.
